Employment

Yorkshire Building Society,
E-commerce Mar 2007 – Present

I joined the E-commerce team at YBS in March 2007 where I took over duties of looking after the YBS groups main websites: Accord Mortgages, Barnsley Building Society, Yorkshire Guernsey, Yorkshire Key Services and Yorkshire Jobs.

Over the past two years I have mainly concentrated on brand strategy and producing a consistent marketing approach for our largest website, ybs.co.uk . This involves adapting promotional content to online media, enhancing user experience, making sales messages consistent across all channels, extensive copy writing and updating the regularly changing sales products – Savings, Mortgage and Insurance accounts. I also work with third party companies to produce effective online sales conversions.

Previous Employment

Lending Support/Lending Systems,
Business Analyst Feb 2005 – Mar 2007

Lending Support was created from other key departments throughout the Society in March 2006, and the aim was to offer support to mortgage processors with a new mortgage system.
